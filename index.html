Brian Dyer

Brian Dyer is a dedicated multimedia designer and developer who connects artists with their audiences. Knowledgeable in both HTML and CSS, he's coded websites for bands Itsy Bitsy and The Fjord People. With a passion for brand development, Brian has also designed color palettes, managed social media pages, and edited live videos for various clients.

Brian works at Boise State Public Radio, editing a variety of radio promos in Adobe Audition that go on-air and can be heard every day. His drive for music and audio engineering has led him to utilize software such as Audacity, Logic, and Studio One Pro to create music as daft quest. Additionally, Brian is the drummer for Itsy Bitsy and is featured prominently on their self-titled EP, released in December 2024.
